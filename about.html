Introduction

Qur-aan is the word of Allaah. Allaah Has sent Qur-aan through Angel Jibreel [Gabriel] (peace be upon him) to Muhammad (peace and mercy of Allah be upon him). This is a platform to read the last ten surahs of the Qur-aan along with surah Al-Faatiha and listen to their recitations.
